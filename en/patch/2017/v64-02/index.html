2017.10.19 patch notes (v64.02) | TERA KR

Update

  • Adjusted the amount of points earned via Uncommon quests
    • Eyes Wide Open
    • Crack the Crustacean
    • Bad Day at Bleakrock
    • Tuwangi Reflexes Training
